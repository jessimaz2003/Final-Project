Mental Health

Keeping your mind healthy is essential for success. The University of Michigan offers numerous resources to help you stay healthy and focused during hard times:

 

Student Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

University CAPS: Access resources and assistance to ensure positive mental health.

 

University Health Service (UHS)

University Health Service (UHS): Access comprehensive medical care, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, and specialist referrals.

 

Exploring Campus

As busy students, it can be hard at times to find a balance between academics and life outside of assigned coursework. For this reason, the University of Michigan provides students with a variety of leisure spaces where they can get work done while socializing or enjoying a varied atmosphere.

 

Libraries and University Buildings

Take advantage of all the amazing and unique study spots scattered around campus. You will never get bored.

 

Outdoors

Whenever the weather is nice, take advantage and lay a blanket out in the law quad, swing on a hammock in the diag or sit on a bench at the Nichols Arboretum.

 

Tutoring Services

College can sometimes be hard, and asking for a little help at times could be the solution to your main challenges. Here are some examples of resources you could use when facing questions or doubs in your coursework or need additional support:

  • Student Organizations:
    Join one of the many student organizations to connect with peers who share your interests and passions, suppoorting each other.
    •  
  • GSIs and Professors:
    Go to office hours with questions prepared in order to eliminate confusion in assignments.
    •  
  • Career Services:
    Utilize career counseling, job search resources, and networking opportunities to prepare for your professional future.
    •  
  • Tutoring Services:
    Take advantage of the Univerity of Michigan's free tutoring and peer assistance if ever in doubt.
